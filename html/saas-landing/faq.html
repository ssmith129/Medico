FAQ

  2. FAQ

What services do you offer?

We offer a range of healthcare services including X-ray, blood tests, physiotherapy, dental cleaning, nutrition counseling, ENT consultations, skin allergy treatment, and eye checkups.

How do I book an appointment with a doctor?

Yes, simply visit our website and log in or create an account. Search for a doctor based on specialization, location, or availability & confirm your booking.

Can I request a specific doctor when booking my appointment?

Yes, you can request a specific doctor while booking your appointment. If the doctor is available at your preferred time, we’ll be happy to schedule you accordingly.

Do I need to fast before a blood test?

Some blood tests, such as glucose or cholesterol tests, require fasting for 8–12 hours beforehand. Our staff will inform you of any specific instructions when you schedule the test.

Do you accept walk-in patients?

We accept walk-ins depending on doctor availability. However, we strongly recommend booking in advance to reduce waiting time and ensure availability.

How will I receive my test results?

Your test results will typically be shared with you within 2–3 business days via phone or email. Urgent results will be prioritized and communicated sooner if needed..

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, we offer online consultations for select doctors and specialties. This allows you to speak with a healthcare professional from the comfort of your home using your smartphone or computer.

Can I get a prescription after an online consultation?

Yes, if your doctor deems it appropriate, they can provide an electronic prescription after your online consultation. You can then collect the medication from your preferred pharmacy.
